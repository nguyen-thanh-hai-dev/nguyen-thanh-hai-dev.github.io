Tournament Introduction

Previously known as the Vietnam Duel Pro League, this tournament has been the premier Heroes of Magic III competition in Vietnam, bringing together top players from around the world. This year, it has been rebranded as the Vietnam Duel Master 2025.

The Vietnam Duel Master 2025 tournament is the leading event for duel map players in the Heroes of Magic III community in Vietnam.

This year, in addition to Vietnamese players, the tournament will also feature top players from the MDGA competition.

Held online in April 2025, Vietnam Duel Master 2025 is not just a competition, but also a celebration of the Heroes 3 gaming community in Vietnam.

Tournament Rules

  • Format: Use the official   Duel 2.9b   map template created by MKC.
  • Number of players: Up to 100 players based on the registration list.

  • ❖ 2 stages: Group Stage and Playoffs.

    ❖ Group Stage: Top 16 of MDGA are seeded into 16 groups while the remaining players are randomly assigned. Players face off in BO3 matches.

    1st and 2nd places → Upper Bracket, 3rd place → Lower Bracket. Others are eliminated.

    ❖ Playoffs: Double Elimination format. Group winners face random runner-ups in Upper Bracket. 3rd place → Lower Bracket Round 1 vs Upper Bracket Round 1 losers.

    BO3 matches, from Top 8 onward → BO5.

    ❖ Regulations: Common Tournament Rules.

    ❖ Streaming: Required (Twitch, YouTube, Bilibili, or Discord).

    ❖ Reporting: Winner must report in #results channel (break time, Skeletons/Demons).

    ❖ Casting: Announce in #casting if you want to stream a game.

    ❖ Ranked Games: All games must be ranked.

    ❖ Timer: 2 / 1:10 / 0:20. Simultaneous turns: 127.

    ❖ Templates:

    • Upper Bracket: Duel 2.9b, standard trade, 1 Hero pair reroll (500 gold), must reroll / ban previous faction. No ban in BO5.
    • Lower Bracket: Duel 2.9b T+ (Tavern Choice), all random replaces standard trade.

    ❖ T+ Rules:

    • Can hire 1 extra Hero at start. Must remove 1 by turn 111. No more than 1 extra Hero.
    • Choose 1 of 3 available Heroes (1 start + 2 in tavern) to keep as main.
    • Competitive timer: 3 / 1:10 / 0:20.
    • Restarts allowed: 2x111.

    ❖ Note: Organizers may adjust rules depending on tournament needs.

  • The tournament results will be updated at https://challonge.com/vi/VDM25
