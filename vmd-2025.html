Vietnam Duel Masters 2025

Tournament Introduction

The Vietnam Duel Master 2025 tournament is the leading event for duel map players in the Heroes of Magic III community in Vietnam.

This year, in addition to Vietnamese players, the tournament will also feature top players from the MDGA competition.

Held online in April 2025, Vietnam Duel Master 2025 is not just a competition, but also a celebration of the Heroes 3 gaming community in Vietnam.

Register for the Tournament:

For Vietnamese players, please register at the Facebook group: https://www.facebook.com/groups/tieulinhhota/?ref=share&mibextid=WaXdOe

For non-Vietnamese players, please register at the Discord: https://discord.gg/dCwmnDMU

Prizes

Welcome to the Vietnamese crowdfunding tournament, proudly supported by the passionate Vietnamese Heroes 3 Community.

This exciting event is presented by the esteemed Tieulinh HOTA, with the generous sponsorship of Cong Danh Thermal Treatment Inc., our main partner in this thrilling competition.

Prize pool: $1500

Prize for Tournament Records:

  • Earliest break and win the game: $50
  • Most skeletons and win the game: $50 (minimum: 3000)
  • Most demons and win the game: $50 (minimum: 700)

Tournament Rules

  • Format: Use the official Duel 2.9b map template created by MKC.
  • Number of players: Up to 100 players based on the registration list.
  • Detailed tournament rules will be updated later.
  • The tournament results will be updated at https://challonge.com/vi/VDM25
